A Study of Los Angeles Service Provider Performance

Los Angeles, commonly known as LA, is a sprawling metropolis located in Southern California, USA. The population of Los Angeles was over 3.8 million people in 2022, making it the most populous city in California and the second-most populous city in the United States after New York City. The Greater Los Angeles Area, which includes surrounding counties, is home to around 18 million people.

In the era of digital connectivity, internet service providers (ISPs) play a pivotal role in shaping the technological landscape of urban environments. As the second most populous and diverse cities in the United States, Los Angeles stands at the forefront of this digital evolution, where the performance of ISPs becomes a crucial determinant of the city's overall technological advancement. This study seeks to provide a comprehensive analysis of internet service provider performance.
