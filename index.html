"The world is a jungle in general, and the networking game contributes many animals" - RFC 826

The goal of this site is to help spread knowledge and increase every person's understanding of the internet.

Networks and the protocols that have been designed as specifications for how they are to operate and transmit data are often overlooked or taken
for granted by most people. This is expected. In the same way that an old lady doesn't need to know how the electrical components in her dish washer
operate to wash her clothes a regular person often doesn't need to know how their data is successfully transmitted, they just need it to work.
